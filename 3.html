3. It boasts three of the world’s largest shopping malls

Shopping is a big deal in the Philippines. In fact, it boasts three of the world’s ten largest shopping malls: SM Megamall, SM North Edsa and SM Mall of Asia. The biggest mall is the SM North Edsa, measuring an impressive 498,000m2. Put into context, that’s larger than the Vatican City. You’ll find the supermall in Quezon City, in Manilla. Even if you’re not a self-proclaimed shopaholic, it’s still worth the trip to take a peek at the sky garden, simulated river and cinema complex.
