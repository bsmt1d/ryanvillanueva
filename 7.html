7. The national bird is the Monkey-Eating Eagle

The Philippine Eagle is also, quite unfairly, known as the monkey-eating eagle. It doesn’t actually eat monkeys, but its prey isn’t exactly immodest in size — pigs, snakes and bats. With a wing span of around two metres, it isn’t difficult to see how it manages this. It is also considered one of the rarest and most powerful birds in the world and killing one comes at a hefty price — 12 years imprisonment.
