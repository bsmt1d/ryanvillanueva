5. It’s the home of Asia’s first Basketball League

Basketball is a national obsession in the Philippines. It’s thought that the love affair began under American Colonial rule during the early twentieth century, when it was first introduced into the public school system as a women’s game. Today it’s everywhere, from makeshift hoops on street corners to dedicated sports centres. The Philippines professional league is Asia’s first and also one of the oldest in the world — second only to the NBA.
